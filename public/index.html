Chapter One

The Man Who
Carried the Camera

Before the election rallies and party meetings, there was a journalist with a camera and an unwavering conviction: that truth, when told without fear, can change a nation.

For over 20 years, Dipak Bohara walked the corridors of power — not as a politician, but as a watchdog. His investigative reports from News 24, Nepal Television and beyond exposed corruption that others were afraid to touch.

Chapter Two

When the Reporter
Became the Reform

The Rastriya Swatantra Party offered something rare in Nepali politics — a genuine reformist movement led by people who had lived outside the political establishment. For Bohara, it was a natural next step.

2002

Enters Journalism

Begins a distinguished career as an investigative journalist, using media as a tool for accountability and social justice across Nepal.

2013

News 24 – Senior Investigative Reporter

Builds reputation as a fearless reporter over an 11-year tenure, exposing institutional corruption and advocating for press freedom.

2022

Joins Rastriya Swatantra Party

Aligned with Rabi Lamichhane's reform movement, contributing expertise in organizational strategy, election reporting and governance reform.

2025

Elected Joint Secretary – RSP

Appointed to the Central Secretariat and Joint Secretary role, leading organizational management, election preparation and strategic policy coordination.

2026

Candidate for House of Representatives

Standing for Kanchanpur Constituency No. 2 — total vote count shows him with 33,952 votes ahead of established incumbents.

Chapter Three

A Platform
Built on Integrity

His agenda is not a politician's wish list — it is the blueprint of a man who has spent a career documenting what goes wrong when governance fails its people.

Anti-Corruption

Strong accountability mechanisms, independent institutions, and zero tolerance for the misuse of public trust.

Good Governance

Citizen-focused policy making, institutional reform, and reduced political interference in public administration.

Media Freedom

Protection of journalists, independent press, and full transparency in government information systems.

Kanchanpur-2 Development

Infrastructure, employment, and public services that reflect the actual needs of Sudurpashchim's people.

Economic Reform

Private-sector growth, economic freedom, and a business environment that creates opportunity for all Nepalis.

Grassroots Engagement

Direct, transparent communication with citizens — not promises from a podium, but accountability at every level.
